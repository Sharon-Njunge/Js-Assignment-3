    Description

    Anomalor is a simply designed, yet powerful password creator that can also store and manage passwords completely offline! It is stronger than default password creators and your password will not be saved in a database. This means that you're in complete control.


    Enhance and optimize the security of your email accounts, online businesses, bank accounts and most importantly your social identity. Create a powerful password that works for any websites, platforms and apps such Facebook, Google, iCloud, LinkedIn and more.


    By using Linear Congruential Generator algorithm with newly created modulus, multiplier, increment and seed on each usage; we can generate truly pseudo-random numbers and are further transformed using other advanced methods. The strongest, most unique and untraceable passwords are now available at your fingertips.


    Thanks for using Anomalor and making the world a more secure place.
